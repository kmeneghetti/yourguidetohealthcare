Our health is never certain. This may seem like an extremely pessimistic viewpoint, but it’s true! You never know when you’re going to get sick, break a bone, or become hospitalized due to unforseen events. It is really important to have health insurance if you don’t want to be in debt due to illness or other emergencies.
